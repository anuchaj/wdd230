Meet Aba

Sunset at Aba is one of the most beautiful things you will see here.

You can find many places to show your devotion to God.

We know, you know, everyone knows that places to eat is very important, and Aba knows that, so you are able to enjoy different meals in very amazing spots over the whole town.

The place is not the only important thing to eat, the food too, and it's good as the places you can eat.

Aba is known as a growing town, offices are growing too in the zone, the best of all, you have an amazing landscape with a look to the Daule's River.

Malls are really necessary in our days, and this one specially because it will be one of the biggest one in Aba

If you are low of time, plaza are a better options than malls searching for parking lots and that kind of things. Plaza makes your life easier!

To be honest, night activity you will only find on weekends, on weekdays you won't, but still look awesome during the night.

Cutural things can't be missed here, performances are scheduled during the whole year.
