Target Audience:

Virtually anybody can find value in our business. However, it is targeted for those who are looking forward to improve their business, and or those who might be looking for a particular service from a company. Small business owners who want to make their products and services accessible to the people that might be interested in them.

Scenarios:

1. Jozee visits the Chamber of Commerce Site to find out about upcoming networking events where she can connect with other local business owners.

2. Sarah visits the site to find information on business resources such as funding opportunities and business planning tools.

3. A new business owner visits the site to find out about who can register with Aba Chamber of Commerce and local business regulations and requirements.

4. A resident visits the site to find out about what is the goal of Aba Chamber of Commerce and local businesses in the area and to support them.

5. A business owner visits the site to find out about local business services such as marketing and web design.
